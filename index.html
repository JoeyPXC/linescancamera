Line Scan Camera

A "poor man's" finish-line camera: point it at the line, and it builds a scrolling image out of thin slices of video over time — the same idea as a real photo-finish camera, just built from ordinary video frames instead of a true high-speed sensor. Good for reading order and rough timing casually, not official results.
